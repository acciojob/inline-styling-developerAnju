Flowers photos

Flowers are usually the most colorful part of a plant.Flowers can grow separately or together in an inflorescence. Flowers play an important role in ecosystems by attracting pollinators and providing food for animals. Flowers have been used medicinally for thousands of years. Some flowers have religious or historical significance. Some flowers are carnivorous and trap insects to digest them. Flowers can hear buzzing bees. Flowers are thermogenic and can generate heat to attract pollinators.

Roses pictures

A rose is either a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa in the family Rosaceae or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars. They form a group of plants that can be erect shrubs, climbing, or trailing, with stems that are often armed with sharp prickles.
